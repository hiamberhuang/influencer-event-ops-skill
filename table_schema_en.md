# Table Schema (English)

## 01 WBS Master

Controls the whole project: phase, workstream, task, output, deadline, owner, status and risks.

## 02 Rundown

Each segment must explain why it exists, what happens on site, and who owns it.

## 03 Venue & Flow

Compare venue quotes and record site visit findings. Do not rely only on supplier photos.

## 04 Creator List

Prioritize creators and manage invitation rounds: longlist, soft invite, second invite/backfill, final invitation.

## 05 Budget

Track planned budget, forecast, actual, variance, payment method, payment status, handler and vendor/payee.

## 06 Duties & Rehearsal

Every on-site action needs an owner and backup. Rehearsal must be physical.

## 07 Seating

Seat numbers should connect table cards, wristbands, raffle IDs, transport cards and the sign-in sheet.
